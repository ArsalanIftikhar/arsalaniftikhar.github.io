AI QA & Coaching Case Study (Call Centre)

Status: Consulting engagement delivered and concluded (Jan 2026)

Engagement: NexAura consulting (Jul 2025–Jan 2026) | Focus: QA visibility, scalable scoring, and coaching dashboards.

Context / problem

QA teams needed a scalable way to review call quality and make coaching interventions visible across teams. Manual review processes could not keep pace with call volume or consistently surface development themes.

Pipeline overview

  • Ingest calls from agreed sources.
  • Transcribe interactions for structured analysis.
  • Apply rubric-based scoring aligned to QA criteria.
  • Publish dashboards for QA and operational coaching visibility.

Requirements translation + KPI definitions

  • Converted QA and operations expectations into explicit KPI definitions and scoring logic.
  • Documented scoring criteria to support consistent interpretation and coaching usage.
  • Added audit-friendly validation checks so outputs could be reviewed and challenged when required.

Outcomes

  • Delivered a working MVP for QA/coaching visibility across call-centre workflows.
  • Improved transparency of scoring logic via documented KPI and rubric definitions.
  • Completed engagement handover with supporting documentation and validation approach.
