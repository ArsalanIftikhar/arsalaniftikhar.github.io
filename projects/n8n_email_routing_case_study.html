AI-Powered Email Routing Workflow

An intelligent automation using n8n and OpenAI to automatically parse, categorise, and route incoming emails with reliability, consistency, and scalability.

Project Type

AI & Automation

Core Technologies

n8n, OpenAI, Email APIs

Sector

Cross-Industry

Impact

Consistency & Scalability

The Business Challenge

Organisations receive large volumes of inbound messages daily — whether customer support requests, HR enquiries, or operational reports. Manually triaging these is time-consuming, inconsistent, and can expose sensitive data if staff rely on consumer AI tools. A scalable, automated, and secure solution is needed to categorise, prioritise, and route communications effectively.

The Solution: An AI-Powered Triage Agent

I built a prototype workflow in n8n that integrates with OpenAI to reliably parse and route incoming emails. The workflow enforces structured prompts, validates schema outputs, and applies routing logic to ensure scalability and security. This approach demonstrates how automation and LLMs can be combined to streamline everyday business processes.

The Automated Workflow:

1. Trigger & Data Capture

The workflow is triggered by new incoming emails (but could equally be CRM updates, form submissions, or file drops). Key details such as subject, sender, and body text are captured and parsed into variables.

2. AI Categorisation & Prioritisation

The message data is passed to an OpenAI model with a structured prompt enforcing JSON-style outputs. This ensures reliable categorisation, prioritisation, and summarisation while balancing creativity and determinism through controlled temperature settings.

3. Schema Validation & Error Handling

Generated outputs are validated against a defined schema. Invalid responses are flagged and reprocessed, ensuring robustness and preventing malformed data from progressing further into the workflow.

4. Conditional Routing & Response

Based on categorisation, the workflow applies conditional logic to route messages to the appropriate team or generate an automated response to the sender, ensuring fast and consistent communication.

Key Outcomes & Business Impact

  • Consistency: Every message is categorised and prioritised against the same rules, reducing human bias and error.
  • Security: Managed integrations ensure data is handled safely within organisational systems, avoiding risky use of consumer AI tools.
  • Scalability: One workflow can handle thousands of requests with minimal intervention, making it suitable for high-volume environments.
  • Flexibility: The trigger and routing logic can be adapted for multiple domains — customer support, HR, compliance, or operations.
  • Exploration & Innovation: This prototype highlighted the practical potential of combining workflow automation with AI for real-world business processes.
